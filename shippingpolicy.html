Service Delivery Policy

Digital Services - No Physical Shipping

Digital Services: As a creative services provider specializing in photography and videography, we provide digital content only. No physical shipping is involved in our service delivery.

1. Delivery Method

All final photographs, videos, and edited content are delivered digitally through secure online methods:

  • Secure download links via cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox, or similar platforms)
  • Online galleries for easy viewing and downloading
  • Digital file transfer as per client preference

Delivery timelines are clearly communicated and agreed upon before project commencement. We strive to meet all delivery deadlines as promised.

2. Revisions & Edits

Revisions are an important part of ensuring client satisfaction:

  • Revisions are provided strictly as per the agreed scope outlined in the service agreement
  • The number of revision rounds will be specified in your contract
  • Additional revisions beyond the agreed scope may incur extra charges
  • Re-shoots or additional photography sessions will be billed separately

3. Completion of Service

A service is considered officially completed when:

  • All final deliverables have been shared with the client
  • Client has acknowledged receipt of the deliverables
  • All agreed revisions have been completed
  • Final payment has been received (if applicable)

Once service is marked as complete, any further modifications will be treated as a new project request.

4. File Retention

We retain project files for a limited period after delivery. Clients are responsible for downloading and backing up their files promptly. Files may be deleted from our servers after 30-60 days post-delivery.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Photography & Videography Industry Specific

1. Booking & Advance Payments

Photography and videography services are resource-intensive and require careful planning. This includes:

  • Resource allocation and team scheduling
  • Equipment preparation and studio setup
  • Manpower coordination and logistics
  • Declining other bookings for the same time slot

Important: Therefore, advance payments are mandatory to confirm bookings and reserve your preferred date and time. This advance is non-refundable in most cases as it compensates for our commitment and resource allocation.

2. Cancellation by Client

If you need to cancel your booking, please note the following:

  • Written Notice Required: All cancellations must be communicated in writing via email
  • Before Project Initiation: If cancellation occurs before the shoot date and no work has commenced, partial refunds may be considered after deducting administrative costs, planning fees, and any expenses already incurred (typically 20-30% of advance payment)
  • After Project Commencement: Once the shoot or production process has started, no refunds will be issued
  • Last-Minute Cancellations: Cancellations within 48 hours of the scheduled shoot date will not be eligible for any refund

3. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after shoot commencement or production start
  • Client unavailability, no-shows, or last-minute rescheduling
  • Delays caused by the client that impact project timelines
  • Dissatisfaction not aligned with originally agreed deliverables and scope
  • Completion of services as per the approved scope and contract terms
  • Weather-related issues or force majeure events (we will offer rescheduling options)
  • Subjective creative differences when deliverables meet professional standards

4. Rescheduling Policy

We understand that plans may change:

  • Rescheduling requests must be made at least 7 days before the scheduled shoot
  • One free rescheduling is allowed; subsequent changes may incur fees
  • Rescheduling is subject to availability of our team and resources

5. Refund Processing

Processing Timeline: If a refund is approved based on the terms outlined above, it will be processed within 7–10 working days. Refunds will be issued via the original payment method used during booking.

Please note that bank processing times may vary, and it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to reflect in your account.

6. Dispute Resolution

In case of any disputes regarding cancellations or refunds:

  • Please contact us immediately to discuss the situation
  • We will review your case individually and fairly
  • Our goal is always to reach a mutually satisfactory resolution
  • All decisions will be communicated in writing via email
